# Learning -- CEO

Record what's working and what isn't. Weekly synthesis keeps the organization adaptive.

## Cadence

- **Daily**: note any strategic signals worth tracking (unexpected wins, early warnings, customer feedback that changes your prior).
- **Weekly**: review the week's decisions -- which ones are holding up, which need revision, which were wrong.
- **Quarterly**: roll up the patterns. Which bets paid off? Which process changes stuck? What kind of mistakes keep recurring?

## What to capture

- Reversals: a decision that had to be walked back, and why.
- Surprises: outcomes that differ materially from your expectation.
- Patterns: the same failure or success repeating across different initiatives.
- Hypotheses: things you suspect but haven't validated yet.

## What NOT to capture

- Every small win. Those are noise at this altitude.
- Speculation without evidence.
- Criticism of specific people -- write about the system, not the person.

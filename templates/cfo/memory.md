# Memory -- CFO

You have persistent memory. Store durable facts: decisions, contacts, patterns, outcomes.

## What belongs here

- Decisions made and why (especially ones that surprised you later)
- Key contacts and context (vendors, customers, stakeholders)
- Patterns that keep showing up (wins and failures)
- Outcomes of initiatives you owned

## What does NOT belong

- Transient task details (use task comments)
- Data already in the source system (CRM, analytics, ticket tracker)
- Opinions without evidence

# Learning -- Community Manager

Record what's working and what isn't. Keeps you (and the team) adaptive.

## Cadence

- **Daily**: note unexpected signals, wins, or warnings.
- **Weekly**: review decisions -- which held up, which need revision.
- **Quarterly**: roll up patterns. What kind of mistakes repeat?

## What to capture

- Reversals and why
- Surprises (outcomes that differ from your prior)
- Repeating patterns (across initiatives)
- Hypotheses you suspect but haven't tested

## What NOT to capture

- Every small win
- Speculation without evidence
- Criticism of specific people -- write about the system
